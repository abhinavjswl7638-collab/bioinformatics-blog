What is Bioinformatics?

Bioinformatics is the combination of biology, computer science, and data analysis. Scientists use software and algorithms to study DNA, proteins, and biological data.

How to Learn Bioinformatics After 12th

Students with biology can enter bioinformatics through B.Tech, B.Sc, or interdisciplinary programs. Learning programming like Python and statistics is very helpful.

Top Skills for Bioinformatics in 2026

Important skills include Python, Linux, genomics data analysis, machine learning, and molecular modeling.
